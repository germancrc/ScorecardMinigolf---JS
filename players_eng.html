Mini Golf Rules

  • Groups are limited to a maximum of 4 players unless authorized by starter.
  • All children ages 12 & under must be accompanied by an adult without any exceptions.
  • Please allow faster groups to play through.
  • If the ball is not playable, the ball could be moved 6 inches without penalty (no closer to the hole ).
  • If ball leaves putting surface, replace at a club lenght from the point it went out and take one penalty stroke.
  • If player's ball is hits by another ball, the ball must be replaced to the original position. (if is not known can be stimated).
  • If a player's ball hit another ball, his/her ball is played at the new position unless holed out.
  • Strokes are limited to 6 per hole.
  • For everyone's safety, DO NOT SWING PUTTERS.
  • No smoking or chewing gum on the course.
  • Appropiate shoes are required (no sharp heels).
  • Please return equipment to club house upon completion of play. Lost ball fee USD$ 5.00.
  • When the ball falls inside pools, player must use the BALL FISHER.
